Why it matters
<p>Perhaps because of the presentation's vagueness, I'm reading into it what I would like it to be.
</p><p>But I think it's more than that: What Wadler is looking to do is to unify queries, interaction, message passing, and HTML/XML generation into a clean, sound programming environment.  These sorts of things are currently handled with a large set of ad hoc imperative languages communicating in poorly-defined ways, leading to a very error-prone system.</p>
